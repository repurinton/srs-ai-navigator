# Society of Robotic Surgery submission

## Title

**The Robot Is Ready. The Hospital Isn’t: AI Transforming Hospital Operations**

## Objective

Demonstrate how six governed AI levers—Digital Front Door, Clinical Diagnosis, Precision Medicine, Robotics, Longitudinal Care, and Task Automation—can be sequenced to surface and relieve operating constraints across a hospital, shifting executive decisions from isolated tool adoption to closed-loop operating-model redesign.

## Brief abstract

Using an interactive hospital operating twin, this session follows a robotic episode across access, diagnosis, readiness, surgery, recovery, and longitudinal care. As each AI lever materializes, the current constraint clears and the next becomes visible, showing that AI value comes not from isolated automation but from coordinating decisions, workflows, and accountability across the care system. The illustrative demonstration concludes with a 90-day approach to prove one closed-loop flow with one owner and one causal scorecard.
